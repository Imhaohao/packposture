Sam is a sophomore at Pine Crest High School in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. His skill sets lie in marketing and customer relations, which he gained from founding his non profit Kids4Kids Relief. Madison is a senior enrolled in the IB Diploma Program at St. John’s School in Vancouver, Canada. She is experienced in leadership, graphic design, and marketing, skills fostered through founding Glow Cosmetics and serving as vice president for Cluborate, a nonprofit entrepreneurship network. In her spare time, you can find her winning dance competitions, editing her school newspaper, or scoping out new cafés with her friends. Madison hopes to continue her entrepreneurial journey to have an unambiguously large, positive impact on society.

His hobbies include playing soccer and badminton, competing in track and field, and travelling.
